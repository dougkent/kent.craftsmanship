Kent Craftsmanship

Kent Craftsmanship is a privately owned woodworking company in the heart of Tomball, TX. After many years of a sparked interest in woodworking, Doug Kent began his journey in building fine furniture.

His fine furniture building began out of necessity as he served on local and international mission trips. His initial portfolio included church pews, picnic tables, and bunk beds... as well as increased creativity and flexibility in times when a Haitian 2X4 actually measured out to be a 3X5! With a passion for precise and handcrafted pieces, Doug shifted from mission trip furniture into the realm of beautiful, fine finished creations. Each piece is handcrafted with expertise and personalized through every detail.

We hope you enjoy the beauty of Kent Craftsmanship's work, and feel free to follow @KentCraftsmanship on Instagram for detailed pictures and videos on the building process.
