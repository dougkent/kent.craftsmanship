Mission Trip Furniture

One of my passions is travelling on mission trips, and in particular building furniture in service to these communities. On a number of occasions, I had to build different furniture pieces in bulk with help from the mission team and from the local population.

When trying to plan projects for these trips, I looked online for a hub of easy-to-build furniture designed for mission trips. I was not able to find a single one. As a website developer in my professional career, I was uniquely able to create such a hub.

Linked below is missiontripfurniture.org where users can go and freely search for plans as well as create a free account and upload their own plans to share.
